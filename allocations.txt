Income allocation percentages (as defined in income.js monthly budget logic):

- Mom: $100 flat (or reduced when total < $170)
- Spending: 20% of total income (minimum $70 unless total < $170, then special rules)
- HYSA: 35% of remaining after Spending + Mom
- IRA: 25% of remaining after Spending + Mom
- Fidelity: 15% of remaining after Spending + Mom
- Checkings: Whatever remains after the above allocations
